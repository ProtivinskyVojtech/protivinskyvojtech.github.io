Twenty One Pilots

Hailing from Ohio, Twenty One Pilots—comprised of Tyler Joseph and Josh Dun—have reshaped the music landscape with their genre-blurring sound since their formation in 2009. Blending elements of alternative rock, pop, hip-hop, and electronica, the duo has captivated audiences worldwide.

Their breakout album, "Blurryface," catapulted them to stardom with chart-topping hits like "Stressed Out" and "Ride." The album's exploration of anxiety, identity, and modern life resonated deeply, showcasing their ability to infuse infectious melodies with introspective lyrics.

Known for their electrifying live performances, Twenty One Pilots combines energetic stage presence with intimate moments, showcasing their musical versatility and powerful storytelling.

Continually evolving, their subsequent albums like "Trench" and "Scaled and Icy" demonstrate their willingness to experiment while maintaining their signature authenticity. Their music touches on themes of self-discovery and resilience, inviting listeners to embrace their individuality.
